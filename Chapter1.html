Chapter 1 – The Great News

The Divine Fusion That Created Humanity

Chapter Excerpt

“Adam is not the first man. Adam is the divine Nafs — the sacred breath of God — that fused with the evolved Bashar to give birth to Al-Insān.”

This chapter opens with the Quranic verse "Qul huwa nabaʾun ʿaẓīm" — “Say: It is a Great News.” That News is not a prophecy, but a concealed truth: that Adam is not a biological figure, but the divine Nafs itself — God’s own breath. When this Nafs was fused with the self of an evolved Bashar, the result was a new dual being: Al-Insān.

Key Insight

Humanity is not descended from a single physical ancestor, but is the ongoing product of a metaphysical event: the fusion of Bashar (a self-aware but limited being) with Adam (the divine Nafs). This chapter reinterprets Quran 2:30 not as a creation myth, but as an announcement of this transformative act — the divine elevation of life into consciousness through breath.

وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى جَاعِلٌۭ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ خَلِيفَةًۭ
“And when your Lord said to the angels: I am placing a successor on Earth.” (Quran 2:30)
