Disclaimer

The Great Revelation is the product of independent study and sincere reflection on the Quran. It does not claim affiliation with any religious institution, sect, or traditional school of interpretation.

The ideas presented in this work are rooted solely in the Quran’s own language and structure, without reliance on any external sources, or inherited doctrines. Readers are invited to explore the content with openness, rigor, and personal discernment.
